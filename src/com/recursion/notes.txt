
Recursion**********************
------------------------------

1. Method calling itself directly or indirectly
2. Types of Recursion.

         |- Indirect Recursion .

             A() --->B()---->C()----->A()

        *|- Direct Recursion .

              |- Tell  Recursion
              |- Head Recursion
              |- Tree Recursion

3. If we stop the recursive calling , we can use Base Condition .

 Tell  Recursion
 -------------------

1. If a recursive calling statement is thr last statement in the method , so we can say that  Tell  Recursion
2. Approach :  Top-Down Approach
